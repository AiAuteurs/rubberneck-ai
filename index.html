FAQ

How does the process work?

Submit your spot, name your price, share your concept. We review it personally and come back to you within 24 hours. If we're aligned, you pay 50% upfront and we get to work. First cut in 48 hours after brief approval.

What do I need to provide?

Your logo, any mandatory brand assets, and your brief. If you have a concept, bring it. If not, we can develop one — quoted separately. The more you give us, the better the first cut.

What does "48 hours" actually mean?

48 hours from the moment your brief is approved — not from when you submit. We align on the direction first, then the clock starts. No surprises, no moving goalposts.

How do revisions work?

Two rounds included in every project. A revision round is one consolidated set of feedback in a single communication. Round three and beyond are quoted before we begin. No open-ended revision loops.

What formats do you deliver?

16×9, 9×16, and 1:1 are included as standard. That covers broadcast, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Additional formats or custom aspect ratios are available and quoted separately.

Can I cancel after we start?

Once production begins, cancellation is not permitted and the upfront payment is non-refundable. We invest real creative resources from brief approval. If you need to pause before we start, contact us immediately.

What's the founding member offer?

The first 10 clients name their own price. We review every submission — if we can make it work at your number we will, if not we'll come back with a counter. Either way, let's make something great. In exchange, founding members provide a testimonial we can use on the site.

Do you work with any brand?

We're selective. We take on work we believe in and brands we think we can genuinely help. Submitting a spot is an application, not a purchase order. We'll tell you honestly if we're the right fit.
